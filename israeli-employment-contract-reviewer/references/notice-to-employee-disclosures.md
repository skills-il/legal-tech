# Notice to Employee Law: Mandatory Written Disclosures

Source: Notice to Employee and Job Candidate (Employment Conditions and Screening/Hiring Procedures) Law, 5762-2002 (`חוק הודעה לעובד ולמועמד לעבודה (תנאי עבודה והליכי מיון וקבלה לעבודה), התשס"ב-2002`).

The employer must give the employee a written notice of employment terms within 30 days of the start of work (within 7 days for a minor under 18). A signed contract that already contains all the items below satisfies the duty. Use this list as a presence check during a contract review.

## The Mandatory List

When reviewing a contract, confirm each of these appears. A missing item is a finding (major if it is a pay or hours term, minor otherwise):

| # | Item | Notes |
|---|------|-------|
| 1 | Identity of the employer and the employee | Full legal names / company number |
| 2 | Start date | And, for a fixed-term contract, the duration of the term |
| 3 | The role | Main job and a summary of the job description |
| 4 | Total pay components and the payday | All wage components (base + supplements) and when salary is paid (e.g., by the 9th of the month) |
| 5 | Basis of pay | Monthly salary, hourly, weekly, or output (productivity) |
| 6 | Normal working hours | Length of the normal workday or workweek |
| 7 | The weekly rest day | Which day is the employee's weekly rest |
| 8 | Social-benefit contributions | Pension / provident fund payments and the bodies they are transferred to |
| 9 | Applicable extension orders / collective agreements | Any extension order (tzav harchava) or collective agreement that applies to the role |

## How to Use This in Review

1. Walk the contract once against the table above, ticking each item.
2. For each missing item, add a finding: a missing pay component or hours term is a major finding (it is a statutory disclosure duty); a missing softer item (role summary, applicable extension order) is a minor finding.
3. If the contract is missing several items at once, recommend the employer reissue a complete written notice rather than patching clause by clause.
