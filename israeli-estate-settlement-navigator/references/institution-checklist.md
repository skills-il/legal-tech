# Institution-by-institution checklist

The Israeli-specific notification and document map. Work top to bottom, but skip
rows that do not apply. Misrad HaPnim forwards the death to many bodies
automatically, so for several rows your job is to confirm and to claim, not to
be the first to tell them.

| Institution (He) | Institution (En) | What to do | Documents | Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| רשות האוכלוסין וההגירה | Population and Immigration Authority (Misrad HaPnim) | Confirm the death is registered; order the death certificate + several certified copies | הודעת פטירה from the doctor/hospital | Certificate is free; it is the master document everyone else asks for |
| חברה קדישא / מועצה דתית / עמותת קבורה אזרחית | Chevra Kadisha / religious council / civil burial society | Arrange burial | הודעת פטירה, deceased ID | Bituach Leumi pays the burial grant directly; do not pay for standard burial |
| ביטוח לאומי | Bituach Leumi | Confirm they know of the death; actively claim survivor benefits | תעודת פטירה, claimant ID | Benefits are NOT automatic; eligibility belongs to israeli-survivor-benefits-navigator |
| בנקים | Banks | Notify; expect the account to freeze; cancel standing orders; request a statement | תעודת פטירה, sender ID; later the order | Funds released only against the succession/probate order |
| קופות גמל / פנסיה / חברות ביטוח | Pension, provident funds, insurers | Notify; ask about beneficiary payouts and claim forms; use the clearing house (mislaka) to locate accounts | תעודת פטירה, claimant ID | Some payouts follow beneficiary designations rather than the estate |
| מעסיק | Employer | Notify payroll/HR; claim final salary, notice pay, unused vacation, severance | תעודת פטירה, sender ID | Also ask about workplace-managed pensions and insurance |
| קופת חולים | HMO (kupat cholim) | Deregister the member | תעודת פטירה | Stops membership charges |
| חברות תשתית (חשמל, מים, גז) | Utilities (electricity, water, gas) | Close or transfer the account holder; issue final bill | תעודת פטירה, sender ID | Transfer is often easier than closing if the home stays occupied |
| עירייה / ארנונה | Municipality / arnona | Change the registered holder; ask about a survivor-linked discount | תעודת פטירה, occupancy proof | Discount tied to survivor benefits; confirm at the municipality |
| משרד הרישוי | Licensing Bureau (Misrad HaRishui) | Transfer the vehicle | תעודת פטירה + the order; spouse may have a simplified route | Do after the order is obtained. The license lapses on death (renewed for relatives one year only); confirm mandatory insurance with the insurer before anyone drives |
| משטרה / האגף לרישוי כלי ירייה | Israel Police / firearms licensing division | Deactivate a licensed weapon through the licensing bureau to keep it, or surrender it to the police within 30 days of death | כלי הירייה, תעודת פטירה, רישיון כלי הירייה של הנפטר | The personal firearms license lapses on death; the police issue a deposit confirmation |
| לשכת רישום המקרקעין (טאבו) | Land Registry (Tabu) | Re-register real estate to the heirs | תעודת פטירה + the order + Land Registry extract | Do after the order is obtained |
| סלולר / אינטרנט / מנויים דיגיטליים | Cellular, internet, digital subscriptions | Cancel to stop recurring charges | תעודת פטירה, sender ID | Include streaming and app subscriptions on standing orders |

## Sequencing reminders

- Get the death certificate and several certified copies before starting the cascade. Everything downstream needs it.
- Before notifying anyone, run the two free government asset-locators by the deceased's ID: Har HaKesef (הר הכסף, Ministry of Finance) for lost pension savings, life-insurance policies, and bank accounts, and Har HaBituach (הר הביטוח, Capital Market Authority) for all insurance policies. This surfaces accounts and policies the family did not know about.
- Bank freeze and asset release are two different moments: freeze happens on notice; release happens only against the succession or probate order.
- The order itself (צו ירושה / צו קיום צוואה) is obtained separately: route to `israeli-wills-inheritance`.
- Survivor benefits are a separate, claim-based track: route to `israeli-survivor-benefits-navigator`.
