# ILPO Fee Schedule Summary

Source: Israeli Patent Office (ILPO) official fee schedule, Ministry of Justice.
Reference URL: https://patents.justice.gov.il (the older host israelpatents.justice.gov.il still resolves)

Fees are denominated in NIS and are updated annually in line with the Consumer Price Index (CPI). The figures below reflect the 2026 schedule. Always verify current amounts on the official ILPO portal before filing.

**Key structural point**: substantive examination is **included in the national filing fee** (and in the PCT national phase entry fee). There is no separate "examination fee" for a normal application. A separate fee applies only when the applicant requests **accelerated** examination.


## 40% Fee Reduction

A 40% reduction applies to:

- **Individual inventors** (natural persons filing in their own name)
- **Companies** with annual turnover (gross revenue) under NIS 10 million in the most recent completed fiscal year

To claim the reduction, file a signed declaration of eligibility at the time of paying each fee. The declaration cannot be applied retroactively. If an applicant's turnover crosses the NIS 10 million threshold during prosecution, the reduction no longer applies to subsequent payments.

The 40% reduction does NOT apply to:
- Attorney or agent fees (private, not set by ILPO)
- Late payment surcharges
- Opposition or appeal fees (check current schedule)

## Examination Fees

A normal application is examined under the filing fee. There is no stand-alone examination fee.

| Fee | Full (2026, NIS) | With 40% Reduction (approx.) |
|---|---|---|
| Filing plus accelerated examination requested by the applicant | 1,191 | see official schedule |

Accelerated examination is requested on an accepted ground (applicant age or health, competing product, basis for other applications, green technology, or Patent Prosecution Highway).

## Maintenance (Renewal) Fees

Israel uses a lump-sum payment at grant for years 1-6, then 4-year advance payments thereafter.

| Period | Full (2026, NIS) | With 40% Reduction (approx.) |
|---|---|---|
| Years 1-6 (paid at grant) | see official schedule (roughly NIS 950 to 1,000) | see official schedule |
| Years 7-10 (paid before year 7 anniversary) | 1,921 | see official schedule |
| Years 11-14 (paid before year 11 anniversary) | 2,882 | see official schedule |
| Years 15-18 (paid before year 15 anniversary) | 4,803 | see official schedule |
| Years 19-20 (paid before year 19 anniversary) | 6,725 | see official schedule |

**Late payment surcharge**: a surcharge applies during the 6-month grace period. After the grace period, the patent lapses and reinstatement requires a separate petition. Confirm the current surcharge rate on the official schedule.


## PCT International Phase Fees (Filed via RO/IL)

PCT international phase fees are set by WIPO and paid in NIS at the Bank of Israel rate on the payment date. They include the WIPO international filing fee, the search fee of the chosen ISA, and the ILPO transmittal fee. The ISA/IL search fee changes periodically (it moved to a new amount effective March 2026); confirm the current search fee on the WIPO and ILPO portals. The 40% reduction applies only to ILPO-set fees, not to WIPO international fees or ISA search fees.

## Notes on Payment

- Payments can be made online via ILPO's portal or by bank transfer to the Ministry of Justice account.
- Foreign applicants must pay in NIS. The Bank of Israel official rate (https://www.boi.org.il/en/markets/exchange-rates/) applies on the payment date.
- Payment confirmations should be retained as proof; ILPO may take several weeks to update its records.
- VAT does NOT apply to official ILPO fees; it applies only to attorney/agent fees billed by private practitioners.
