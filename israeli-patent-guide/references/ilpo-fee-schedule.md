# ILPO Fee Schedule Summary

Source: Israeli Patent Office (ILPO) official fee schedule, Ministry of Justice.
Reference URL: gov.il/en/departments/ilpo

Fees are denominated in NIS and are updated annually in line with the Consumer Price Index (CPI). The figures below reflect approximate 2025 levels. Always verify current amounts on the official ILPO portal before filing.


## 40% Fee Reduction

A 40% reduction applies to:

- **Individual inventors** (natural persons filing in their own name)
- **Companies** with annual turnover (gross revenue) under NIS 10 million in the most recent completed fiscal year

To claim the reduction, file a signed declaration of eligibility at the time of paying each fee. The declaration cannot be applied retroactively. If an applicant's turnover crosses the NIS 10 million threshold during prosecution, the reduction no longer applies to subsequent payments.

The 40% reduction does NOT apply to:
- Attorney or agent fees (private, not set by ILPO)
- Late payment surcharges
- Opposition or appeal fees (check current schedule)

## Maintenance (Renewal) Fees

Israel uses a lump-sum payment at grant for years 1-6, then 4-year advance payments thereafter.

| Period | Full (NIS) | With 40% Reduction (NIS) |
|---|---|---|
| Years 1-6 (paid at grant) | ~2,000 | ~1,200 |
| Years 7-10 (paid before year 7 anniversary) | ~1,200 | ~720 |
| Years 11-14 (paid before year 11 anniversary) | ~1,600 | ~960 |
| Years 15-18 (paid before year 15 anniversary) | ~2,200 | ~1,320 |
| Years 19-20 (paid before year 19 anniversary) | ~1,400 | ~840 |

**Late payment surcharge**: 25% added during the 6-month grace period. After the grace period, the patent lapses and reinstatement requires a separate petition.


## PCT International Phase Fees (Filed via RO/IL)

PCT international phase fees are set by WIPO and paid in NIS at the Bank of Israel rate on the payment date. Key components:

| Fee | Approx. NIS Equivalent | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| International filing fee (WIPO) | ~4,500 | Reduced for e-filing |
| Search fee (depends on chosen ISA) | ~2,500-8,000 | ISA/IL lowest; ISA/EP highest |
| Transmittal fee (ILPO) | ~300 | Fixed by ILPO |

The 40% reduction applies only to ILPO-set fees (transmittal fee), not to WIPO international fees or ISA search fees.

## Notes on Payment

- Payments can be made online via ILPO's portal or by bank transfer to the Ministry of Justice account.
- Foreign applicants must pay in NIS. The Bank of Israel official rate (boi.org.il) applies on the payment date.
- Payment confirmations should be retained as proof; ILPO may take several weeks to update its records.
- VAT (18% as of January 2025) does NOT apply to official ILPO fees; it applies only to attorney/agent fees billed by private practitioners.
