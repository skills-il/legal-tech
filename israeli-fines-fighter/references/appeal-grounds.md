# Appeal Grounds Reference

## Parking Fine Appeal Grounds (עילות ערעור על דוח חניה)

### Procedural Grounds
- **Missing signage (שלט חסר):** No parking sign was present or was placed too far from the violation location. Municipal regulations require clear signage within reasonable distance.
- **Obscured signage (שלט מוסתר):** Parking sign was blocked by vegetation, construction, or another sign, making it unreadable to a reasonable driver.
- **Incorrect details (פרטים שגויים):** The ticket contains wrong vehicle plate number, color, make, or model. Even a single digit error in the plate number invalidates the ticket.
- **Outside enforcement hours (מחוץ לשעות אכיפה):** The ticket was issued outside the posted enforcement hours shown on the parking sign.
- **Defective meter (מדחן תקול):** The parking meter or pay station was not functioning, and no alternative payment method was reasonably available.

### Substantive Grounds
- **Medical emergency (מצב חירום רפואי):** The driver or a passenger experienced a medical emergency requiring immediate attention, making proper parking impossible.
- **Loading/unloading (פריקה וטעינה):** Commercial vehicles actively loading or unloading goods are generally permitted brief stops in no-parking zones (varies by municipality).
- **Valid disabled permit (תג נכה בתוקף):** The vehicle displayed a valid disabled parking permit (tav necheh) issued by the Ministry of Transport.
- **Vehicle breakdown (תקלה ברכב):** The vehicle experienced a mechanical failure that prevented moving it from the location.

## Traffic Fine Appeal Grounds (עילות ערעור על דוח תנועה)

### Owner Liability Defenses
- **Vehicle sold (הרכב נמכר):** The vehicle was sold before the violation date. Requires sale contract with date and buyer details.
- **Vehicle stolen (הרכב נגנב):** The vehicle was reported stolen before the violation. Requires police report with date.
- **Different driver (נהג אחר):** The registered owner was not driving. Owner must submit a statutory declaration (tatzir) identifying the actual driver within 90 days.

### Technical Defenses
- **Camera calibration (כיול מצלמה):** Speed cameras must be regularly calibrated. Request calibration records from the police. If calibration was overdue, the reading is unreliable.
- **Unclear photo (תמונה לא ברורה):** The camera photo does not clearly show the license plate or the violation.

### Circumstantial Defenses
- **Emergency (מצב חירום):** Driving to a hospital or fleeing danger. Requires documentation.
- **Police direction (הוראת שוטר):** A police officer directed the driver to perform the action that was later cited as a violation.

## Legal References

- **Parking fines:** Municipal bylaws (chukei ezer ironi'im) per municipality
- **Traffic fines:** Traffic Ordinance (pekudat hatnu'a) - New Version, 1961
- **Points system:** Traffic Regulations (takanot hatnu'a), 1961, Part 14A
- **Appeal process:** Criminal Procedure Law (Alternative Enforcement - Administrative Offenses), 5746-1985
